How It Works

Who is Jack?

Jack is who you call when you need a hand getting something done. We are a service that connects customers with the growing pool of instantly available workers in the sharing economy for the immediate completion of simple tasks.

Users will be able to connect with the closest or highest rated member of an active pool of workers through the app, at will, for a consistent fixed rate. Once the job is completed both the user and the worker will be able to rate each other and payment will be processed automatically at the end of the job.

What kind of Tasks?

Any simple task you’d ask a friend or personal assistant for help with.

General labor

Anything that can be done accomplished a standard set of basic tools

Cleaning

Anything that can be cleaned with a basic set of cleaning materials

Personal Assistance

Basic office work & organization. No tools or training

Errands

Anything that requires the pick up or delivery of goods

Roadside Assistance

Change Tires, Jump Battery, Deliver Gas. No Tow.
